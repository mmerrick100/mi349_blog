Fantasy Football Week 5 Results

My week 5 matchup was against my friend Matt. I was 3-1 and in 3rd place heading into this week. Once again, I was favored to win the matchup. My team was projected to score 153 while his team was projected to score 147. Before the games started, I must admit I was a little nervous about how the week would go because of how strong Matt's team is. A win against Matt would prove I have one of the better teams in the league, and would make me the favorite to win the entire league.

My team once again outplayed their projection and scored a whopping 194. My MVP this week was Josh Allen becuase he outplayed his projected point total by a very large total. Other notable performances were put on by Derrick Henry, Nick Chubb, Dawson Knox, and Emmanuel Sanders. Matt's team ended up being very tough competition, but I was able to sneak away with the 194-184 victory.

Looking ahead towards week 6, my team is 4-1 and is in second place in the standings. I match up against a sub .500 team (Joe S's team). I am fairly confident that my team will win this game. Stay tuned for next week's results.
